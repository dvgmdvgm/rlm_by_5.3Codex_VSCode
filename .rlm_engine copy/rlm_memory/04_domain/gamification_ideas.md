# 🎮 Scenica Gamification Strategy

This strategy is focused on **user retention**, **encouraging high-quality content**, and **driving subscription sales** through gamification principles.

---

## 🚀 1. Profile Level System (Progression)

**Goal:** Encourage artists to complete their profiles 100% and keep them up to date.

### "Career Growth" Mechanics
Instead of a boring "Profile Strength: 80%", use statuses:

| Level | Requirements | Reward | Impact on Sales |
|-------|--------------|--------|-----------------|
| **New Talent** | Registration + 1 photo | Basic search presence | — |
| **Rising Star** | 5 photos + Video + Bio | +10% visibility boost | Free, but requires effort |
| **Pro** | 3 verified reviews | +30% visibility, "Pro" badge | Hard to reach without a paid push |
| **Maestro** | **Subscription Only** + High rating | Top of search, priority in applications | **Direct status sale** |

**Psychological hook:** "You are stuck at 'Rising Star'. Become a 'Maestro' to get hired faster."

---

## 🔥 2. Streaks and Daily Rewards (Retention)

**Goal:** Drive daily logins even when there are no new jobs.

### "Daily Spotlight" Mechanics
- **Action:** Log in every day for 7 consecutive days.
- **Reward:** Get **1 free "Boost"** for your profile for 24 hours (usually costs money).
- **Streak Break:** If you miss a day, start over. Or... **"Restore your streak for $1"**.

### "Hot Streak" Mechanics
If an artist responds quickly to messages (within an hour), they receive the 🔥 **Fast Responder** badge.
Employers love this badge. Artists fear losing it.

---

## 💎 3. Internal Currency (ArtCoins)

**Goal:** Create an internal economy within the platform.

- **How to Earn:**
  - Refer a friend (+10 coins)
  - Share profile on Instagram/LinkedIn (+5 coins)
  - Get a 5-star review (+50 coins)
- **How to Spend:**
  - Push an application to the top of an employer's list (20 coins)
  - Unlock one employer's contact info (100 coins)
  - Subscription discount (500 coins = -10%)

**Direct Monetization:** Not enough coins? **Buy an ArtCoins pack**.

---

## 🏆 4. Leaderboards and Competitions (Social Proof)

**Goal:** Appeal to artists' egos.

### Weekly Tops
- **"Top 10 Dancers of the Week"** (on the home page).
- **Criteria:** Profile views or new reviews.
- **How to get there:** Buy a Boost or be highly active.

### Badges (Achievements)
Visual rewards for the profile:
- 🌍 **International**: Worked in 3+ countries.
- 🚀 **Early Adopter**: Registered in the first 3 months.
- 🤝 **Verified**: Passed passport verification (paid option or complex KYC).

---

## 🔒 5. FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)

**Goal:** Drive subscriptions through curiosity.

### "Who viewed my profile?" (Classic LinkedIn style)
- **Free:** "3 employers viewed your profile this week. One of them needs an acrobat in Dubai."
- **Blurred:** Company logos and names are hidden with a blur effect.
- **Paid:** "Buy Premium to see who they are and message them first."

### "Your application was viewed, but..."
- When an employer views a free user's application, the artist receives: "Your application was seen! But you are ranked 15th on the list."
- **Paid:** "Subscribe to Pro to always be in the top 3."

---

## 🎭 6. Gamification for Employers

They need to play too.

- **"Trusted Employer" Status**: Awarded if the employer pays on time and responds to everyone. Artists are more likely to apply to these.
- **Scouting XP**: "100 profiles viewed". Shows that the employer is active.

---

## ⚡ Quick Start (MVP)

Start small:

1.  **Profile:** Add a completion progress bar ("Fill in 2 more fields to appear in search").
2.  **Badges:** "Verified" (manual issuance) and "Top Rated" (automated).
3.  **Blurring:** Show that the profile is being viewed, but hide the details (FOMO).
